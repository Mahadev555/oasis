BILL GATES

“Computers and games don’t waste time – people do.”



William Henry Gates III is an American business magnate, software developer, investor, author, and philanthropis
He is a co-founder of Microsoft, along with his late childhood friend Paul Allen.


Here is a timeline of Bill Gates's life events:

  • Gates was born and raised in Seattle, Washington October 28, 1955
  • At 13 he enrolled in the Lakeside School, an exclusive preparatory school.
  • Gates graduated from Lakeside School in 1973. He scored 1590 out of 1600 on the SAT[18] and subsequently enrolled at Harvard College
  • In 1975, he and Allen founded Microsoft in Albuquerque, New Mexico.
  • The Open Letter to Hobbyists was an open letter written by Bill Gates, the co-founder of Microsoft, to early personal computer hobbyists
  • Nov 1 1978 Microsoft opens first international office in Japan
  • Jan 1 1979 Microsoft moves headquarters to Bellevue, Washington
  • 1980 Microsoft partners with IBM to wrtie the BASIC for the IBM PC
  • Jun 25 1981 Microsoft becomes incorporated and Bill Gates becomes President
  • Since 1987, Gates has been included in the Forbes list of the world's wealthiest people.
  • 1989 Bill Gates founds Corbis Corbis Corporation is an American company, based in Seattle, Washington
  • Jan 1 1994 Bill Gates marries Melinda French
  • May 1998 United States v. Microsoft was a set of consolidated civil actions filed against Microsoft Corporation pursuant to the Sherman Antitrust
  • Jul 2 2005 Live 8 concerts
  • Jun 27 2008 Bill Gates Steps Down from Daily Duties
  • As of October 2022, Gates had an estimated net worth of US$107 billion, making him the fifth-richest person in the world

He is one of the people that constantly shape the 21st century
